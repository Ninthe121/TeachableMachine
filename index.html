Mission Briefing

Your goal is to outsmart the opposing agent by choosing the right pose at the right time.

During the 0‑0‑7 countdown, your move is locked in when the third indicator under “7” turns green.

At that moment, your current pose is registered.

You start with 0 bullets and can hold a maximum of 3 bullets in your magazine.You can only shoot if you have at least 1 bullet, so reloading is essential.

The mission ends immediately when an agent is hit or if you shoot without any bullets.

Possible Outcomes

Shoot vs Reload → Reloading agent loses

Shoot vs Shield → Shooter loses the bullet, shield is safe

Shoot vs Shoot → Both lose a bullet

Shield vs Shield →Nothing happens

Reload vs Reload → Both earn a bullet

Reload vs Shield → Reloader gets a bullet
